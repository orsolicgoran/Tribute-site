Nikola Tesla

The man who invented electricity

Time line of Tesla's life:

  • 1856 - Born in Smilja, Croatia
  • 1873 - Finished a 4-year term in only 3 years
  • 1879 - Tesla's father dies of an unspecified illness
  • 1883 - Constructed his first induction motor , after work hours
  • 1884 - Emigrated to the USA and sold his patent of alternating-current dynamos to George Westinghouse
  • 1891 - Invented the Tesla coil, an induction coil widely used in radio technology
  • 1893 - Used an alternating current system to power the World's columbian Exposition
  • 1943 - Died in a hotel room in New York City, all alone
